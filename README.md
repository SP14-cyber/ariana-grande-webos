# ariana-grande-webos

My sister is a huge Ariana Grande fan, so I built her ArianaGrandeOS: an operating system that runs entirely in the browser, themed around Ariana and her music. It's made out of love and perfect for fans! :)

👉 https://sp14-cyber.github.io/ariana-grande-webos/GrandeOS.html 👈

---

## 🎵 What's inside?

I started with a simple pink desktop and ended up building 12 different apps into this thing. It actually feels like a real operating system now:

- The Music Player: Has all 111 songs from Yours Truly to Eternal Sunshine. It has a real custom queue system so you can pick what plays next instead of just listening in album order.
- Trivia App: A fun little quiz game about Ariana’s music and career.
- Fortune Cookie: Click on it to crack it open and get an Ariana-themed fortune message.
- Paint App: You can actually draw whatever you want and save it to your computer as a PNG.
- Customization: You can resize all 4 corners of any window, change fonts, tweak text sizes, and change themes.
- Plus: A browser with her socials pinned, a weather app based on your location, a notes/journal app, a calendar, a to-do list, a stopwatch/timer that beeps, a notification bell, and an intro guide.
- Home Screen: Has a bigger clock and a quote section that rotates through 12 different Ariana quotes every 30 seconds.

---

## 🛠️ Behind the Scenes (How I built it)

This was built for the Hack Club Stardance Challenge. It took about 14 total hours of coding. 

- The Tech: Just pure HTML, CSS, and plain JavaScript.
- The Album Art Struggle: Ariana's album covers are square, but I needed wide versions to fit a desktop screen. I had to manually edit the backgrounds in a photo editor to make them wide. I also had to compress them like crazy so the whole project stays under 2MB and loads fast.
- The Music Sync Issue: Getting 111 songs to sync up properly with a custom queue system was way harder than I thought. At one point, the play buttons broke and audio stopped working entirely. After a ton of testing, everything finally plays perfectly.

Seeing my sister's reaction when she clicked through everything and loved the music player made all the frustrating bugs 100% worth it!

## 🚀 How to run it locally

If you want to download the code and mess with it yourself:

1. Clone this repo: `git clone https://github.com/SP14-cyber/ariana-grande-webos`
2. Open the folder.
3. Double-click `GrandeOS.html` to open it right up in your browser.
